Bio

I am a scientist at AWS AI. I earned my Ph.D. degree in CS at UC San Diego, where I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Rajesh K. Gupta and Professor Jingbo Shang. I also spent some wonderful summers in MIT, UC Davis, and AWS AI Labs. I received my B.S. degree in CS with honors from Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University.

My research interests lie in machine learning for sequential data (NLP, time series, spatiotemporal), with practical impact including healthcare, IoT, climate science, smart city and e-commerce.
